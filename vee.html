Wakulima is a place of yo dream


WAKULIMA is a Non Government Organisation formed in 2019. We are an umbrella organization that coordinates and promotes the development of mordern farming among farmers, distributors and consumers through networking and information distribution. It sees development of the farming sector as a crucial factor for sustainable livelihoods and envisions establishing a vibrant, sustainable and mutually beneficial agriculture sector in Tanzania.
